My Multilingual ABCs

Graphic Design

An illustrated, educational alphabet book helping children learn the pronunciation of food in different languages.

I based my book's theme on food as I was feeling hungry upon reading the assignment details for my graphic design class. Not many realise that nowadays many children are raised in a multilingual environment, so I wanted to fill that gap by using words from different languages instead of English as I was one of those kids. I have illustrated the front and back cover and a cartoony, cute piece of food for every letter of the alphabet. I made the illustrations with Photoshop and the book layout was put altogether with InDesign. As this is a multilingual book, I did my best to include as many different languages as I could with Spanish, Portuguese, Japanese, and French to name a few.
